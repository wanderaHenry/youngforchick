Is an urban brooder that is targeting youth between 18-30. It was founded by youth Member of Parliament
to facilitate young urban dwellers to stimulate their wealthy creation and money economy involvement.
Business
Selling chicks on request; layers and broilers for both local and exotic chicks. The stock of the chicks is
recorded and updated whenever new stock is ready by blooder manager.
Each starter young farmer is supposed to receive 100 chicks only. Whereas returning farmers can take
more chicks but not exceeding 500.
Chicks’ prices are fixed at UGx. 1650 only irrespective of the type of the chicks and each farmer is
eligible to 2 bags of feeds payable after 2 two months.
Blooder Manager records chick stock and approves the requests for the chicks, once chicks are approved,
then sales agents update registers it as a complete sale.
Users
Blooder Manager
He records and updates chick stock, (number of the chicks, type of the chicks, period of the chicks in
days, etc)
Receives the request for chicks from young farmers and approves those requests. On approving the
request, the stock number of chicks reduces.
Sees all young farmers who have taken their chicks
Sales Rep.
These are the 3 individuals who are at the offices to attend to call-in requests (receives calls from young
farmers for chicks and then record those requests).
They also receive all requests and their updates from the manager

Customer
This is the youth between 20-30 years old in the constituency, they are registered with a recommender’s
NIN number captured. (name of the youth, age, gender, recommender’s name, recommender NIN
No.,youth NIN No. contact etc.)
Requests for chicks. A young farmer is supposed to fill in a form requesting for the chicks once in 4
months (name of the youth, number of chicks, type of chicks, type of the farmer (starter/returning),
among other details are recorded.)
Once submitted, they can keep follow up with their request online to see if it is approved by the brooder
manager and ready for picking.
Please note; only registered young farmer are eligible for chick request
