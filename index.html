About Young4Chicks

Young4Chicks is an initiative founded by youth Member of Parliament to facilitate young urban dwellers to stimulate their wealth creation and money economy involvement through poultry farming.

We provide:

  • High-quality chicks (broilers and layers)
  • Starter packages for new farmers
  • Training and support
  • Feed supply system
