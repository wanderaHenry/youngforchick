Welcome toYoung4Chicks

Step into the world of urban poultry farming with this powerful video from Young4Chicks. In just a few weeks, witness the amazing transformation of chicks into strong, healthy birds—raised with care, consistency, and youthful determination. This video isn’t just about poultry; it’s about hope, purpose, and opportunity for young people ready to change their financial future. You'll see how our young farmers, guided and supported by the program, nurture their chicks in a clean, well-managed environment using quality feeds and modern brooding practices. The birds thrive, and so do the farmers—many of whom started with no experience but now see real income from their efforts. Young4Chicks provides more than chicks—we provide access to resources, training, and a community of like-minded youth all working toward one goal: economic independence. With affordable starter packages, a simple request process, and guaranteed support, we empower youth aged 18 to 30 to take the leap into agribusiness confidently. This video captures the spirit of transformation—not just of the birds, but of the lives behind them. Whether you're a first-time farmer or a returning grower, this is your chance to be part of a movement that’s redefining urban agriculture. Join Young4Chicks and hatch a business that grows with you.
